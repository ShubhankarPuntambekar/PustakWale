About Us

PustakWale, an amalgamation of unique and innovative way of books reaching people, we take pride in taking books closer to people helping readers and non-readers to interacte actively with books, start bonding over them and hence spur a vivid yet concrete disscusion over topics they might have or had found interest in. We are bringing this experirence to your housing complexes, to your workplaces, to your kids schools and educational institutes since 2020.

To do this amazing work we are continuously joined by youthful people who help us deliver this joyous experience, currently PustakWale is a team of 50+ young book lovers who have joined us under our Earn & Learn program.

PustakWale has reached thousands of people in and around Pune since its inception making books readily available in housing complexes, schools, educational institutes and even corporate organisations. We believe books carry a special place in our lives and hence with a vision TO strengthen the cultural fabric of the society through literature and arts we PustakWale are on a mission TO setup a mobile book shop at every Arts and Cultural Centre, Housing Society, Schools, Educational institutes and Corporate organisations.
